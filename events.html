Billy the Van is a 1968 English Austin ice cream van who faithfully

takes Ruby Violet ice cream all over the UK. He can be hired for

special occasions such as weddings and birthdays, for corporate

events or simply a party with a difference. He can be filled with a

variety of flavours, personalised wafers, cones, napkins and can be

accompanied by members of staff with usherette trays. Billy has had

the pleasure of serving ice cream at a wonderful variety of events,

to everyone who loves ice cream. We pride ourselves on ensuring all

our events are as personal and as bespoke as possible, please contact
